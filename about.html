About Me

Hey there. Thanks for checking out my 'About Me' page. My name is Chanice Ward. My first name is pronounced 'Shah-nees.' It means the Lord is gracious. It is also a combination of two of my grandmothers' names: Charlene and Janice.
I come from a family of hard-workers, business owners, musicians, and creatives.

I was born in Augusta, Georgia and consider both Georgia and New York home. Home for me is also Glory City Aiken, which has a special place in my heart. It's also where I'm a part of a worship team and help run sound as an audio engineer.

Growing up, I wanted to be a pediatrician and travel around the world and help set up medical clinics. By the time I was 15, I wanted to be a screenwriter. I ended up studying film and video in college; and later fell in love with songwriting.

Being creative -- it's in my DNA. From writing poetry, to publishing books, to creating journals, to capturing photographs while sightseeing -- they all make my heart smile.

Aside from being creative, I heart connecting with people. To be able to connect with others is a gift.

Whether it's working as part of a team, going on adventures, learning something new, brainstorming ideas, finding solutions, or finding ways to make things and processes more efficient for others,
these are all things I believe I was created to do.

I was born to create and to be a blessing.

And to be able to use technology to help improve other peoples' lives (and to see their dreams come to fruition) is another blessing.
